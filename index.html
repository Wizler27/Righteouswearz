Righteous Wearz

Founder – Clarens “Wiz” McCormick

Clarens “Wiz” McCormick was born in the poorest city in the streets of Haiti. Growing up surrounded by poverty, he developed a strong desire to help those in need.

After being adopted to the United States at the age of 7, Wiz was shocked to see that people in America suffered from poverty as well. His faith in Jesus later convicted him to start Righteous Wearz with the purpose of earning income to bless the poor and uplift his community.

Wiz believes that in the Kingdom of God, the greatest are the ones who are humble, least, and poor. Staying kingdom-minded, his passion is to plead the cause of the poor and needy—providing clothing, food, and support to those who are often overlooked but honored in the eyes of God.
