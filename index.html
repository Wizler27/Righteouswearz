Meet Our Founder

Clarens Wiz McCormick was born in the poorest city in the streets of Haiti. Growing up seeing the poverty around him, he knew he wanted to help those who are poor. When he got adopted to the U.S. at the age of 7, he was shocked to see poor people suffering in America too. His faith in Jesus convicted him to start Righteous Wearz in hopes of making money to make a difference for the poor and inspire others to live godly lives.
