About Us

Righteous Wearz is a prophetic covering for the righteous remnant — a brand that recognizes God’s people above all others. When you wear Righteous Wearz, you declare that you are a person of God, righteous, not wicked, and one who seeks to thrive in love, peace, and godliness.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is simple: The brand speaks for itself. We aim to do what the Lord commanded: feed the hungry, clothe the naked, love your neighbor, and help the sick.

Our vision is to cover the earth with a prophetic covering that recognizes people who are morally correct and living in godliness.
